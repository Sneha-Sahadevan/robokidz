Help Desk

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about enrollment, safety, equipment, and coding pathways.

Does my child need prior coding experience?

Not at all! We start our younger group paths (ages 6–9) with visual blocks that require no typing skills. We guide them step-by-step into syntaxes as they gain confidence.

Do we need to buy any hardware kits?

All necessary electronics, chassis units, sensors, and computers are provided for student use inside our labs. If you wish to build at home, we sell take-home kit boxes at our lobby counter.

Are the electronics safe for children?

Absolutely. We use low-voltage DC currents (5V–9V powered by AA batteries or USB power). There is no high-voltage AC exposure, and no soldering is done by children in junior modules.

What is the duration of each batch class?

Regular pathways meet once a week on weekends for 2 hours. Weekend workshops and special holiday intensive camps run daily over designated schedules.
